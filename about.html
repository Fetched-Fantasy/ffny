About Me

Fetched Fantasy is a vibrant network of VRChat communities built around creativity, storytelling, and shared experiences. What started as a single hub has grown into a chain of themed groups—each with its own focus, from immersive roleplay and movie nights to social hangouts and 18+ spaces. At the heart of Fetched Fantasy is a commitment to safety, inclusivity, and community-driven fun. Our official staff team, help desk, and ticket support ensure a professional and welcoming environment, while our Discord and website keep members connected and informed. With over 1,600 members across VRChat and a thriving Discord hub of 370+, Fetched Fantasy is more than just a group—it’s a living ecosystem where imagination, friendship, and events come together. Join us, explore our worlds, and become part of the story.
